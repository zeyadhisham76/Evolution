Trap Music

Trap music beats are a subgenre of hip-hop characterized by,fast, rolling hi-hats, heavy sub-bass from the Roland TR-808 drum machine, and snappy snares. They typically feature a half-time feel, often produced at 130–160 BPM, with a dark, minimalist, and menacing sound.

"The fast paced hi-hats"

Experimental Music

Experimental music is a genre-agnostic approach that pushes musical boundaries by challenging traditional compositional, aesthetic, and performance conventions

"The abnormal patterns and sounds"

Old school Music

Old-school hip-hop, stylized as old skool (originally known as disco-rap) is the earliest commercially recorde hip-hop music and the original style of the genre. It typically refers to the music created around 1973 to 1983

"The parent of hip-hop"
