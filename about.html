I. The Background

"December 2020, that's when I found myself making my first ever beat and of course, like every other skill people learn, it was hard in the beginning Never thought it was going to be easy and I was getting burnt out very fast"

II. The Improvement

"Everything started to be more clear after 2 years, that's when I started to show off my beats felt more confident showing the work I conducted and of course the praise was not enough and still I lacked something that I felt would elevate me as a Producer"

III. The Journey

"I have met a lot of music producers along the way, some of them helped me with their ideas and notes, some of them I just kept taking notes from, tried making a remix for someone and I was shut down tried to release some of my work on social media, never gained the reach I imagined I get, but now it's different"

IV. The Purpose

"Now, my skills have evolved, that "something" that I lacked 4 years ago, I am now aware of it People don't understand that some pupils don't have limitations, don't know how to give up I have got a lot of things I want to achieve and I will start from here, didn't put a finger on a name I would give to myself, but definetly I found a convenienent name for my page to show off my work"

V. The Evolution

"Evolution is a page where I will show my work to the public on an official website, people are tired of the same artists they hear everyday, they need something fresh and clean, various genres and types of music available in one website,and the Purpose is to evolve and dominate the game"
